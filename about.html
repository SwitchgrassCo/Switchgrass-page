About

Switchgrass Solutions builds legislative engagement platforms designed for people who work in and around the policy process. Our tools simplify complex legislation, streamline constituent communication, and create structure where legislative work is often noisy.

Our platforms are used by organizations, policymakers, and advocacy teams that need more than raw volume. By structuring engagement correctly, we help surface patterns—how positions differ by district, which arguments are driving participation, and how sentiment shifts over time.

We create solutions where simplicity meets impact—easy enough for any constituent to use, powerful enough to drive real policy outcomes.
